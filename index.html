What is healthy&bio ?

Healthy&bio is a french brand created in France on 2019 by Dior Diouf. The idea came from a desire to eat healthy food but it was cold outside. So I told myself that it would be good to be able to have healthy products and bio products delivered while not moving from my sofa.
